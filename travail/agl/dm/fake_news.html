Fake News

by Cerisara Nathan

All media are becoming more and more popular over time, especially the internet, but many people misuse it. And there are also a lot of people who don't understand everything on the internet. These people, and others, are gullible and believe all that they read or listen on Internet, or in other medias like radio.

But why internet and others medias are not totally reliable ? And how to know if a news is true ? It's that we will see here. Thanks to read Nath's news post ;)

First, Because anyone with internet access and a little basic knowledge can post anything they want to post on the internet. There are no rules, no regulations, no guidelines, no burdens of proof. And when a person see an information that look authentic, like a good photo montage, made by someone, There is a good chance that this person doesn't verify the sources. But, one other problem, is that in some website, users can have followers and likes, and this users are ready to do anything to make the buzz, to be popular, to have more and more followers and likes. In addition, we must add to this all the hoaxes, the pranks ...

There isn't miracle solutions, if you want to check the veracity of an information, you must check the sources, but if you have a minimum of common sense, you are already able to check a large part of the information in the medias, because these informations are exagerated, stupids, devoid of common sense... First, you must already look at the site or the way of communication of the information, If it isn't serious, don't believe it. Next, you can research in Internet, if you find easily a lot of serious websites that confirm this information, your can believe in it, but, if not, but, if not, you must be careful with this information, it may be false.
